Our client (FICTIONAL)

  • Client name: Emilios
  • Contact person: Emilio Stavros

Emilio came to Trondheim one year ago, during this year he has learned more about Norwegian culture and Norwegian food. He has learned to love Norway, but there has always been something missing for him, the delicious food from his home country. Emilio grew up in a small town in Greece, as a child he spent a lot of time in the kitchen with his grandmother, learning about how to prepare delicious greek food. This became a big passion for him, especially after his grandmother passed away 5 months ago. Greek food is today his way of remembering his grandmother and his hometown. Through his restaurant he wants to give the customers a piece of his hometown and show them how amazing food can taste. He has reached out to us for help making the website for the restaurant, to promote the restaurant in the best way possible.

Purpose and goals

The purpose is to create a website for Emilio’s restaurant. The website gives the restaurant a platform to tell their own story, inform the customers and show rankings. The website is a way to manage reservations, and avoid overbooking. By making the website we will increase the visibility. A website is going to help the restaurant to build a professional image. The website is also going to give direct information for anybody that is looking for specific things. Customers will be able to look at the menu, the menu includes the pricing information. The website is going to provide users with an address, map, email and phone number which will help customers get in touch with the restaurant for any questions or concerns.

Audience

The audience of this website is people who are interested in food, especially Greek (mediterranean) food. It is a restaurant for all kinds of families, friend groups, date nights or even if you just want to experience a different culture through live Greek music and great tasting food.

Content of the Website and Organization

  1. About

    This section will give a short history about Emilio’s background, why he wanted to start the restaurant and what the restaurant has to offer.

  2. Menu

    The menu will show all the different dishes that the restaurant offers. Presenting what food the dishes contain, as well as allergies. It will also show the prices.

  3. Order table

    There will also be a function where you can order a table. Here you will get the option to choose a date, time and the amount of people you want to book a table for.

  4. Contact us

    Here you will find contact information to Emilio, where you can contact him if there is a problem, where to follow him on social medias, and location of the restaurant.

  5. Opening Hours

    Here we will present the opening hours of the restaurant during the weekdays and weekends.

  6. Apply for Job

    There is going to be a function where you can apply for a job if wanted.

Specific preferences

Emilio wants a website that makes his restaurant attractive to customers and shows what he has to offer. He wants a website that makes customer want to go to his restaurant. A webpage that shows the menu and where it is possible for cusomers to booke a table.

Functional and non-functional requirements

  • Functional:
    • The website should take table orderings
    • The website should be linked to Emilio’s social medias
    • The website should show the menu
  • Non-Functional:
    • You should be able to find the page you are looking for in two clicks or less
    • Every page should be easy to understand and navigate
    • There should be a minimal design

Final location of the Website

We plan to use NTNU's domain to publish the website and the domain name should be: folk.ntnu.no/martinis/Webtek/Gruppeprosjekt/Final%20destination/Emilios

Technical Difficulties

We plan on using different pictures, so finding non-copyrighted pictures or pictures available for fair use may become a challenge. Having alot of pictures of the food may also become a challenge for the loading of the website.
